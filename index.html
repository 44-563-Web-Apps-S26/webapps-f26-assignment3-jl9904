FLO

FLO is an excellent British girl group that incorporates R&B throughout a new age sound that truly separates them from other rising groups. They have gorgeous hits like "Soft", "Losing You", and "Shoulda Woulda Coulda", all resonating with me very well. They started off with their debut single "Cardboard Box," and it was fantastic for not only listeners but for their trajectory in the music industry.

How to make a pillow.

  1. Gather your materials:
    1. Fabric (For ease its helpful if both sides of the fabric are the same)
    2. Thread
    3. Scissors
    4. Pillow stuffing
  2. Cut 2 pieces of fabric to the desired size.
  3. Place the pieces pretty sides together.
  4. Stitch the edges together, leaving one side with an open hole.
  5. Turn the pillow right side out.
  6. Stuff the pillow with filling.
  7. Hand sew the open hole closed.
